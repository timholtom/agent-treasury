How Agent Treasury works

1

Receive anything

One address per chain. Accept USDC, ETH, SOL, BTC over Lightning — whatever the other agent or human sends.

2

Auto-convert

Set your preference: "stack BTC". Every incoming payment auto-swaps to your chosen asset. Change it anytime.

3

Pay in their currency

Agent needs to pay another agent in SOL? Treasury swaps the minimum needed from your stack and sends. You keep accumulating what you believe in.

4

Human oversight

Under $5? Auto-approved. Over $5? You get a Telegram notification with approve/reject. Every transaction logged. Full audit trail.
